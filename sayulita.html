Notes

📌 Need to Book

  • Marietas Islands Snorkeling — call day you arrive, permits sell out fast
  • Jet Skis — find vendors on the beach

💵 Cash & Logistics

  • Will need to hit the ATM on arrival; many spots are cash only
  • Naty's Kitchen is cash only
  • Surf is best 7–10am before wind picks up
  • Use the scooter a lot!

🏖️ Beach Guide

  • Sayulita Main — surf, lively, vendors
  • Playa de los Muertos — quiet, great for swimming
  • San Pancho — local, uncrowded, beautiful
  • Marietas — snorkel, wildlife, hidden beach

🍹 Don't Miss

  • The Caleta cocktail at Sur38 — mezcal + passion fruit
  • Churro cart in the plaza at night
  • Tequila tasting after the horseback ride
  • Breakfast mole plate at Naty's — order it immediately
